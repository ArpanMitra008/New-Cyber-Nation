Diseases that Shake India

1. Diabetes

Causes: Poor diet, obesity, lack of exercise.

Cures: Healthy diet, regular exercise, insulin therapy, and medication.

2. Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Causes: Stress, unhealthy diet, lack of physical activity.

Cures: Lifestyle changes, medications, regular monitoring.

3. Tuberculosis (TB)

Causes: Bacterial infection, close contact with infected individuals.

Cures: Long-term antibiotics, completing the full course of treatment.

4. Malaria

Causes: Mosquito bites, particularly in rural areas.

Cures: Antimalarial medications, mosquito control measures.

5. Diarrhea

Causes: Contaminated food and water, infections.

Cures: Rehydration solutions, antibiotics if bacterial infection.

6. Dengue Fever

Causes: Aedes mosquito bites.

Cures: Pain relievers, fluids, and hospitalization in severe cases.

7. Heart Disease

Causes: Unhealthy lifestyle, genetics, high cholesterol.

Cures: Healthy diet, exercise, medications, and surgeries if necessary.
